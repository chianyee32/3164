Cancer Drug Response Predictor

Drug Cell Line Resistance Prediction Tool using Multi Omics Data

We developed this model as part of our Final Year Project at Monash University Malaysia, with the goal of contributing to the field of precision oncology through data science. This tool is designed to predict chemoresistance in cancer cell lines by leveraging multi omics data, specifically transcriptomic and proteomic profiles. The model focuses on four of the most common cancers in Malaysia: breast, lung, colorectal, and head & neck cancers.

By allowing users to upload drug related identifiers, the system generates predictions on how resistant a given cell line may be to certain chemotherapeutic drugs.

This platform serves a wide range of users, including oncologists, clinical researchers, biomedical scientists, and bioinformatics professionals. It supports efforts in personalized medicine by helping users identify potential non responders to standard treatments, reduce ineffective drug trials, and guide more informed therapeutic decisions.
